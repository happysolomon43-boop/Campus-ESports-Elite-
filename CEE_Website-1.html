About CEE

Not Just a Tournament.
An Institution.

Campus eSports Elite is the first professionally structured competitive gaming league on campus, modelled on the UEFA Champions League Swiss System. No other campus tournament runs this level of complexity or prestige.

Every match is tracked. Every stat is recorded. Every player gets their moment — including a guaranteed ₦500 participation reward, regardless of final ranking.

Season 1 is the beginning. The goal: a registered campus club, inter-university competitions, and CEE as the dominant name in campus esports across Nigeria.

Tournament Format

The Swiss System

01
🎯
Pot Seeding
20 players ranked by skill into 4 Pots of 5. Pot 1 = Elite. Pot 4 = Developing. Every player faces all skill levels — true UCL spirit.
02
⚔️
League Phase
Each player plays 8 matches — 4 Home, 4 Away. 2 opponents from every pot including your own. 80 total league matches.
03
📊
Table Cutoff
Top 4 auto-qualify to QF. Ranks 5–12 go to Playoffs. Ranks 13–20 are eliminated. Tiebreaker: Points → GD → GF.
04
🔥
Knockout Phase
Playoff Round → Quarter-Finals → Semi-Finals → Grand Final. All two-legged except the Final. ET + Penalties if needed.
